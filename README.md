ATV Resource Gauge

A lightweight desktop tool that estimates the token usage, energy consumption, and water usage of your AI prompts in real time. Built as a companion to the Harmonic Calibration Practice (HCP) — a framework for ethical, efficient, and intentional human-AI collaboration developed by Against the Void.
Every conversation with an AI system draws on real physical resources: electricity from a grid, water used to cool data centers. This tool makes those costs visible while you work, so you can develop intuition for what efficient prompting actually looks like — and make that efficiency a habit.
How to use it

Steps to Download and Launch:

1) Click on ATV Resource Gauge.exe
2) On the right, look for the down arrow, which is the  "Download raw file" button, and click it.
3) You will see a warning with an orange triangle and exclamation point. The file is safe; click the ... to the right of the warning, and click "Keep."
4) You will then see a full version of the previous warning. The file is still safe; click the blue ..., and click "Keep Anyway."
5) You will now download the file. From there, just double click the file and it will run.
6) You can drag it to your desktop if you like to launch it from there. You can also pin it to your task bar if you right click, though you may need to click "More Options" first if you don't see the option to. Note: the option to pin to the taskbar may not be available for all versions of Windows. It will launch from the desktop no problem if that's the case.

No installation required. The gauge floats on your screen while you work. Paste any prompt into the text box and it will instantly estimate:

Tokens — your prompt, estimated response, and round trip total

Power — energy consumption in mWh

Water — water usage in mL

The indicator dot shows teal (efficient), gold (moderate), or rose (heavy) so you can calibrate at a glance without reading numbers.
